### [Tarkovsky's films online for free](https://kottke.org/23/05/watch-tarkovskys-best-films-online-for-free)

- Mosfilm, one of the largest USSR film studios, has uploaded full-length versions of many highly acclaimed and influential films on YouTube for free.
- The selection includes six films by Andrei Tarkovsky, which appear on Sight and Sound's 2022 list of the best 100 movies of all time.
- The films available for free streaming include Stalker, Solaris, Ivan's Childhood, The Mirror, Andrei Rublev, and The Passion According to Andrei.

### [Scar tissues make relationships wear out (2013)](https://gist.github.com/gtallen1187/27a585fcf36d6e657db2)

- Relationships are hard to maintain, both personal and professional.
- Scar tissues, i.e., accumulated little annoyances, are the primary reason for failing relationships, not a single catastrophic event.
- Zero lingering animosity is necessary to prevent scar tissues from building up in a relationship. Communication, compromise, and both parties' willingness to listen and compromise are essential to maintain a long-term relationship.
