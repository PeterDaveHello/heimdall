## [Cortex A57: Performance and Power Efficiency for Nintendo Switch](https://chipsandcheese.com/2023/12/12/cortex-a57-nintendo-switchs-cpu/)

- The Cortex A57 is Arm's first 64-bit capable core, used in the Nintendo Switch's Nvidia Tegra X1 for high GPU performance in a portable gaming console.
- It has a modest branch predictor and caching system, a 48-entry instruction TLB, a unified register file, distributed scheduling, and two integer pipelines.
- The A57 processor can handle four integer operations per cycle, but floating-point operations have high latency. It has limited address space, and optimization efforts can improve game performance on the Nintendo Switch and older platforms with integrated graphics.
