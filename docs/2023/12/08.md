## [Lithium Market Plunges as Demand Surges and Spot Prices Collapse](https://wolfstreet.com/2023/11/23/lithium-shortage-bubble-implodes-once-again-as-demand-and-production-both-surged/)

- Battery-grade lithium carbonate spot prices have dropped by 77% in one year due to a shift from a lithium shortage to a surplus, caused by increased production and demand.
- Stardust Power, a startup, intends to construct a lithium refinery in the US using funds from a merger with a Special Purpose Acquisition Company (SPAC).
- The US plans to join the global lithium production market, alongside current dominant players such as Australia, Chile, and China, with Exxon Mobil and other companies preparing for production in the near future.

### [Reactions](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=38556456)

- The article discusses the demand for solar production, potential sodium substitution in batteries, the environmental impact of lithium mining, and the strategic benefits of lithium reserves.
